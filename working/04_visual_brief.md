# Agent 4: Visual Brief

## Asset 1 — Hero Image Prompt

### Image Generation Prompt (for Midjourney / DALL-E / Stable Diffusion)

```
A warm, residential rooftop solar panel installation on a traditional German single-family home (Einfamilienhaus), photographed in golden hour afternoon light. The panels are mounted flush on a south-facing pitched roof with dark roof tiles, with a small German village or suburban neighborhood visible in the soft-focus background. The image feels personal and lived-in, not corporate — perhaps with a garden or terrace visible below.

Natural photography look, slightly warm color grading, slight lens vignette. Clouds are partially visible to add realism — this is Germany, not the Mediterranean. Shot with a wide-angle lens from a slight low angle looking up at the roof. The solar panels have a subtle sheen reflecting blue sky.

Style: editorial, warm photojournalism. NOT stock-photo corporate. NOT sci-fi. NOT futuristic. Real family home.

Aspect ratio: 16:9 (landscape). Crop-safe center 4:3 area should contain the roof and panels.
Resolution: at least 1920×1080px.
```

### Suggested variations:
- Version A: Close-up of panel surface with reflected sunlight, roof tiles in foreground, out-of-focus garden in background
- Version B: Wide shot of house from street level, panels visible on roof, warm morning light, tree partially framing the shot
- Version C: Aerial-ish perspective looking down at panels on roof, showing the panel grid pattern

### Alt text suggestion:
`Solar panels on the roof of a German single-family home in warm afternoon light`
